Jodhpur – The Blue City

Jodhpur, known as the Blue City of Rajasthan, is famous for its striking blue-painted houses, towering Mehrangarh Fort, and vibrant bazaars. The city reflects the grandeur of Rajputana history.

Top Attractions

Mehrangarh Fort – One of the largest forts in India, offering panoramic views of the Blue City.
Jaswant Thada – A white marble cenotaph built in memory of Maharaja Jaswant Singh II.
Umaid Bhawan Palace – A royal palace, part of which is now a luxury hotel and museum.
Mandore Gardens – Historic gardens featuring cenotaphs, temples, and memorials of Marwar rulers.
