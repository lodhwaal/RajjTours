Mount Abu – Hill Station & Lake

Home Jaipur Jodhpur Jaisalmer Udaipur Pushkar Kota Mount Abu Chittorgarh

Mount Abu, the only hill station in Rajasthan, offers a cool climate, scenic lakes, and temples. It is popular for both nature lovers and spiritual seekers.

Mount Abu has been a retreat for centuries, known for its Nakki Lake, ancient Dilwara Temples, and Guru Shikhar peak. The hill station blends natural beauty with cultural and religious significance.

  • Ancient Era: Hill retreats and small settlements.
  • 12th–15th c.: Dilwara Temples built by Jain patrons.
  • Modern Era: Tourism, sunset points, lakes, and trekking attract visitors.

Top Attractions

Nakki Lake – Scenic lake and boating ➕
Nakki Lake is the centerpiece of Mount Abu, offering boating, gardens, and relaxing views surrounded by hills.
Dilwara Temples – Exquisite Jain temples ➕
Renowned for marble architecture, intricate carvings, and spiritual ambiance; a key pilgrimage site.
Guru Shikhar – Highest peak in Rajasthan ➕
Offers panoramic views, sunset photography, and a temple dedicated to Guru Dattatreya.
Sunset Point – Hilltop viewpoint ➕
Popular for sunset views over the Aravalli hills, a must-visit for photographers and tourists.
Best Time: Oct–Mar
Nearest Airport: Udaipur Airport (165 km)
Railway: Abu Road Station (28 km)
Stay: 2–3 days

Suggested Itinerary

1 Day: Nakki Lake → Dilwara Temples → Sunset Point.

2 Days: Day 1 as above, Day 2 Guru Shikhar trek, local sightseeing.
