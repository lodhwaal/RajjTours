Historic Journey of Rajasthan

  • 2600–1900 BCE: Indus Valley settlements at Kalibangan, Balathal.
  • 600 BCE: Matsya Mahajanapada and early settlements.
  • 700–1200 CE: Rise of Rajput polities; forts at Chittorgarh, Ranthambore constructed.
  • 1100s CE: Meena rule and influence across parts of Rajasthan before full Rajput ascendancy.
  • 1559 CE: Udaipur founded by Maharana Udai Singh II.
  • 1700s–1800s: Maratha pressures; treaties with British East India Company.
  • 1947: Integration of princely states into India begins.
  • 1956: Modern state of Rajasthan formed by reorganization.
  • Today: Rajasthan—major tourism, culture, crafts and education hub.
